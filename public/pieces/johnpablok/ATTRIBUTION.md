# JohnPablok Cburnett chess pieces

The piece set under this directory is **"JohnPablok's improved Cburnett chess
set"** by John Pablok — a modified version of the "cburnett" set originally by
Colin M.L. Burnett. Relative to the original, the pawns are slightly smaller
versus the back row and the black pieces carry a white outline so they stay
legible on dark squares.

- **Author:** John Pablok (modifications); Colin M.L. Burnett (original cburnett)
- **License:** [CC-BY-SA 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/)

Two variants ship, both used by the app:

- `flat/`   — no drop shadow
- `shadow/` — with drop shadow

Each variant has the 12 piece SVGs named `wP wN wB wR wQ wK` / `bP bN bB bR bQ bK`.
